There is an increasing trend of people leaving the country of origin to move to other countries around the world. This trend has been the same for Sri Lankans in the past and is quite regular recently due to the economical and political instability within the last few years. When the move occurs, people are often required to leave loved ones, wealth, and ongoing tasks behind resulting the need of support in the areas like,

  • Family connection and care
  • Property maintenance
  • Holiday arrangements
  • Legal documentation
  • Tasks at government departments
  • Social Work

With the limited facilities, infrastructure and technology available in the country, addressing these requirements from a distance might be extremely complicated.

The need for a trustworthy service provider in Sri Lanka to address this complication is now more essential than ever. Here at KithtoKinLanka, our purpose is to provide this service that comforts like family, and is loyal like a friend.

Vision

To help people live where they choose


Mission

To help people with a Sri Lankan origin carry out living abroad with no worry about any responsibilities left behind.

Who we are


KithtoKinLanka (PVT) LTD, is a private limited company registered in Sri Lanka under the companies act No 07 of 2007. We provide service only within Sri Lanka for Sri Lankans living abroad.

Our Community

Our community comprises everyone with a Sri Lankan origin. While we live anywhere in the world, we like to help each other. Helping others is a value in our culture.

Sri Lanka is rich in diversity, whether it is language, religion, culture, gender or even weather! Diversity is our wealth. KithtoKinLanka stands for respecting diversity.

KithtoKinLanka challenges the under-representation of some groups like women and people with disabilities in the Sri Lankan workforce. We give priority to all under-representated people and groups in our business.

We welcome support from anyone in the community to accomplish our mission while maintaining our values, that is, helping each other, inclusion and diversity.
